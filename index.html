GD Fruit List

Community list and user leaderboard.

About the Fruit List:

If you have any questions, ask in our Discord: https://discord.gg/hYZnKqnC

Rules

  1. You MUST have video proof of your completion.
  2. ALL inputs MUST be caused by a fruit (oranges will work best).
  3. Fruits must not be touching any object or organism besides the input device at the time of input.
  4. However, you may be in contact with your orange if you are clicking a dash orb, or are in ship or wave.
  5. Video footage must be unedited.
  6. Any gameplay affecting mods are disallowed except CBF (ex: noclip).
  7. If I google if it's a fruit and it says no, it doesn't count. If it says it is a fruit, it counts. Simple.

Clarifications

  1. 100% noclip is considered completion.
  2. You may use as many fruits as you want.
  3. You may use as many kinds of fruits as you want.
  4. This list is not only for demons; lesser difficulties are fully welcome.
  5. Moderators have final say in ambiguous cases.

Scoring and Placement

  • A demon's initial placement will be determined by the GDDL tier.
  • Points awarded for completion will be determined by the GDDL tier to the power of 1.75.
  • Levels that lean heavily on the Rule 3b clause (i.e., Nine Circles) will have their score lowered slightly.
  • For non-demon levels, the formula is: (x - 1)1.75 / 100, where x is the number of stars the level is worth.
  • This x value can be increased by 0.1 to 0.9 depending on the level’s difficulty.
  • Level placements can be adjusted by moderators if they feel a level is placed poorly.
  • All values before the grand sum are rounded to the nearest hundredth to avoid irrational numbers.
